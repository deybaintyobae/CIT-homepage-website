About Our Department

VISION

We aspire to be a leading innovative, high-performing college that develops technologically proficient graduates.

MISSION

Contribute in the skills development of the Filipino workers Innovatively proficient, productive, and versatile in all aspect of technology evolution Transform students with competence essentials for technological advancement.

Goal 1

Develop qualified students to be effective instruments of inquiry and research with a sense of responsibility and commitment for the improvement of human condition-physical, mental and spiritual domains.

Goal 2

Stimulate and maintain functional and productive activities in various disciplines.

Goal 3

Acquire skills and leadership competence in various disciplines in order to accelerate community progress as trained professional in government and non-government agencies.

Goal 4

Adapt skills and internalize intellectual integrity, moral, cultural, and aesthetic values exemplifying ethical norms and professionalism.

Research Areas

E-Library

A state-of-the-art digital learning hub equipped with high-speed internet, multimedia resources, and collaborative study spaces. Access thousands of academic journals, e-books, and research databases to support your educational journey.

CPT Lab

A cutting-edge computer laboratory featuring the latest hardware and software technologies. Equipped with modern workstations, network infrastructure, and specialized tools for programming, system administration, and technical troubleshooting.

MIS Lab

An advanced computing facility designed for data analysis, database management, and enterprise system development. Features high-performance computers, business software suites, and simulation environments for real-world IT solutions.

CPT Lec

A modern, acoustically-optimized lecture hall designed for interactive learning. Equipped with smart boards, projection systems, audio-visual equipment, and comfortable seating to enhance the educational experience.
